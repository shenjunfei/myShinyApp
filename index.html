Chinese Zodiac

Zodiac derives from the similar concept in western astrology and means "circle of animals". It is a scheme devised to help illiterate people use the Chinese ordinal system and estimate a person's age. Over the centuries, it has evolved into a systematic superstitious plan of future action that relates each year to an animal and its reputed attributes according to a 12-year mathematical cycle. It remains popular in several East Asian countries including China, Vietnam, Korea, Japan, and Thailand.

If you were born in Jan or Feb, this calculation may be incorrect. However, if you were not born in these two months, the calculator has the correct year of animal for you!
